Welcome to Magma Machining Private Limited

Magma Machining Private Limited. is a manufacturing unit established in the year 2004, with the purpose and objective of supplying quality sub-assemblies and assemblies in the process of precision machining parts, press tools, jigs & fixtures related to the Automotive & Aerospace industries, Gas Turbines Parts, Hydro power plant equipment, etc.

Magma lays emphasis on Accuracy, Precision and Commitment.

We are approved in WOC (Work Order Contact) in jobs of flight model components of pay load satellites for space application center (SAC) (Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) Ahmedabad). the company has the entire necessary infrastructure relating to technology, manpower, computers, measuring instruments and accessories. the company has already dealt with super alloy material namely Nimonic, X-22CrMo V121, Inconel 718, Hastelloy, Aluminum 6061, Invar, Covar, Haynes25, AISI403Cb, D2, D3 and Ai 6351, Naval Brass CZ 112, Ti6Al4V Grade5, SS316 Ti, AZ31B.
